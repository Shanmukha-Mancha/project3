This Website is About GeminiAI

Google Gemini is a multimodal AI model that can perform a variety of tasks across different devices and systems. It can understand and respond to multiple types of content, including text, images, audio, video, and programming code. Gemini is Google's most advanced AI model to date and was created in collaboration with Alphabet, Google's parent company." Gemini's models are examples of “multimodal AI models,” meaning that they can respond to a range of content types, such as text, video, audio, and programming code.
